FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

No commission — Quick Game only charges fixed subscription fees.

Yes — cafés may allow Cash on Delivery or accept online payment methods if enabled.

We store minimal customer data needed for bookings and use secure transport (HTTPS) and encryption at rest for sensitive fields.
